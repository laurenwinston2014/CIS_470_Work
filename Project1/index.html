What is Cloud Computing?

The Internet of Things is centered on machines communicating with one another. It is mobile, virtual, and allows for connection from anywhere. The Internet of Things gathers data from sensors and leverages it. It functions using cloud-based applications to analyze and communicate data. One application of this concept is in bridges where sensors can be used to monitor the stability of the bridge to ensure that nothing catastrophic occurs. The article discusses other applications of the Internet of Things and how it can make our world safer and smarter. This shift in technology can bring about smart bridges, smart cars, and soon smart cities. Click here for the full article.

Cloud computing is essentially storing and accessing data and programs over the Internet. This will take the place of a hard drive in a computer. The cloud is part of almost everything on our computers now, and applications such as Microsoft office are beginning to develop versions of cloud computing.
